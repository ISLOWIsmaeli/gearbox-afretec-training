Module 3 Summary

Electronic Components Overview

This week we did a general overview of electronic components.

The electronic components covered include:

  • Resistors
  • Capacitors
  • Inductors
  • Diodes
  • Relays
  • Transistors
  • Quartz Crystal

Resistors

They impede the flow of current

Capacitors

They store electric field

Inductors

They store magnetic fields.

Diodes

Allow current to flow in one direction

Relays

Used to control switches

Transistors

Combination of two diodes

Quartz Crystal

Have a known vibrating frequency maintaining the a regular clock in microcontroller
